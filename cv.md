#Professional Summary#

I am Chuol Ruei Deng, a driven researcher, entrepreneur, and development advocate with a strong foundation in Economics and Development Engineering. As the Founder and CEO of both the Thok Nath Research Institute and the Bangtigow Foundation, I have led initiatives that empower communities through education, technology, and sustainable development. I possess extensive experience in data analysis, research design, and project management, having contributed to high-impact projects in economic development, blockchain research, and refugee education programs.

Passionate about creating meaningful, lasting change, I combine analytical rigor with creative problem-solving to design and implement initiatives that address complex socio-economic challenges. My work spans field research, policy analysis, and capacity-building programs, with a focus on delivering measurable outcomes for underserved populations. I thrive in dynamic, cross-cultural environments, bridging the gap between data, strategy, and actionable solutions, and I am committed to advancing equitable development through innovative and scalable approaches.
