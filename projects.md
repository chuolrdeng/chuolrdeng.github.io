# My Projects

## Bankerom AgriTech
![Bankerom](images/farm.jpg)

Bankerom AgriTech is a startup I founded to empower farmers with **technology-driven solutions**.  
We focus on satellite imagery, smart irrigation, and data analytics to increase productivity and ensure food security in local communities.  
Our vision is to make farming more **efficient, sustainable, and profitable** for smallholder farmers in Africa.

## Thok Nath Research Institute
![Thok Nath Institute](images/research.jpg)

The Thok Nath Research Institute focuses on **language preservation, education, and technology integration**.  
Our current projects include developing a **Nuer-English dictionary**, teaching the Nuer language online, and working with Google Translate to include Nuer (Thok Nath).  
This institute is also building field research documentation on Nuer social, political, and economic organization.
